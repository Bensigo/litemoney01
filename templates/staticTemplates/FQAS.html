FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What is LiteMoney?

LiteMoney is an online wallet which deals with online transactions of any kind such as transfers, recieve & withdrawal of funds.

How it Works?

Once you have registered & your account has been verified. You are expected to purchase a certain value of LMU(LiteMoney Unit) which can be transferred from one user to another with a LiteMoney account. This LMU you have in your account can be used to purchase recharge cards & other items from our affiliate stores.

What is LMU?

LMU stands for LiteMoney Unit & it is the purchasing power used within the LiteMoney Platform/Wallet.

How can you purchase LMU?

You can purchase LMU online within the site in the credit section via your debit or credit card.

Can you send & recieve LMU?

Yes you can. LMU can be send to an account holder by using the unique dentifier attached to his/her account. If you'd also like to recieve LMU you can, by simply providing your unique identifier to the sender

What is LMC?

LiteMoney Credits or LMC for short are reward points that are awarded to a profile each time a deposit is made. These points are awarded based on the value of the deposits.
